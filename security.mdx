# Security

Slinq is designed with security as a priority. Here's how we protect you.

## Smart Contract Security

### Locked Liquidity

Every Slinq token has **permanently locked liquidity**:

- LP tokens are sent to the LP Locker contract
- There is no withdraw function
- Not even the Slinq team can unlock liquidity
- This prevents "rug pulls" where creators steal funds

| Parameter | Changeable? |

### No Team Tokens

Slinq tokens have:
- 100% of supply in the liquidity pool
- 0% team allocation
- 0% pre-mine
- No hidden wallets

The creator only earns through trading fees, not token holdings.

## MEV Protection

### What is MEV?

MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) attacks include:
- **Front-running:** Bots buying before your transaction to profit from price impact
- **Sandwiching:** Bots buying before and selling after your trade
- **Sniping:** Bots grabbing tokens in the first block of a launch

### How We Protect You

Slinq uses dynamic fees that decay over 2 minutes:

| Time After Launch | Fee |
|-------------------|-----|
| 0-30 seconds | 10% |
| 30-60 seconds | ~7% |
| 60-90 seconds | ~4% |
| 90-120 seconds | ~2% |
| After 2 minutes | 1% |

High early fees make MEV attacks unprofitable, giving regular users fair access.

## Fee Distribution Security

### Smart Contract Custody

Creator fees are held by the **Fee Locker** smart contract:

- Not controlled by Slinq team
- Only the creator wallet can claim
- Cannot be redirected or stolen
- Transparent on-chain

### Claim Safety

When you claim fees:
- Only your wallet can initiate the claim
- Funds go directly to your wallet
- No intermediaries or custodians

## Platform Security

### Wallet Connections

Slinq uses **Privy** for secure wallet connections:

- We never see or store your private keys
- Sign-in uses cryptographic signatures
- Session tokens are encrypted
- No password storage on our servers

### Signature-Based Deployment

Token creation uses EIP-712 typed signatures:

- You sign a message, not a transaction
- No gas fees for deployment
- Replay protection prevents duplicate deploys
- Signature proves ownership without exposing keys

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a vulnerability:

1. **Do not** post publicly
2. Contact us through official channels
3. Provide detailed reproduction steps
4. Allow time for us to fix before disclosure

We take security reports seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure.
