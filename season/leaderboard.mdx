# The Leaderboard

The leaderboard ranks all Slinq participants by total Season Pass points. Climb higher to unlock rewards.

## Accessing the Leaderboard

1. Visit [slinq.meme/leaderboard](https://slinq.meme/leaderboard)
2. Connect your wallet to see your personal rank
3. Browse top performers and their point totals

## Leaderboard Features

### Top 3 Podium

The top three participants get special recognition:

| Position | Display |
|----------|---------|
| 1st | Gold trophy, largest display |
| 2nd | Silver medal, featured |
| 3rd | Bronze medal, featured |

### Ranked Table

Below the podium, see:

- Rank number (#4, #5, etc.)
- Wallet address (shortened)
- Total points
- Tier badge (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum)

### Your Stats

When connected, your card shows:

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Your Rank** | Current position |
| **Total Points** | Sum of all points |
| **Creator Points** | From token fees |
| **Trader Points** | From trading volume |
| **Referral Points** | From referral program |
| **Current Tier** | Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum |
| **Progress** | Points to next tier |

## Rank Tiers

| Tier | Points Required | Badge |
|------|-----------------|-------|
| Bronze | 100 - 999 | Bronze medal |
| Silver | 1,000 - 9,999 | Silver medal |
| Gold | 10,000 - 99,999 | Gold medal |
| Platinum | 100,000+ | Diamond |

<Info>
Tier badges appear next to your name on the leaderboard and across the platform.
</Info>

## Update Frequency

| Data | Update Frequency |
|------|------------------|
| Total Points | Every 2 hours |
| Rankings | Every 2 hours |
| Your Stats | Near real-time |

<Tip>
Individual stats update faster than the public leaderboard. Check your profile for the most current data.
</Tip>

## Minimum Threshold

To appear on the public leaderboard:

- Minimum: 0.1 points
- Anyone below this threshold is hidden from public view
- You can still see your own stats when connected

## Common Questions

<Accordion title="How often does the leaderboard update?">
