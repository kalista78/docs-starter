# Season Pass

The Slinq Season Pass rewards active participants with points. Earn points by creating tokens, trading, and referring friends.

## Current Season

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Season** | Genesis Season |
| **Start** | December 5, 2025 |
| **End** | April 5, 2026 |
| **Duration** | 4 months |

<Info>
Points accumulate throughout the season. At season end, top participants receive rewards based on their rank.
</Info>

## Rank Tiers

Your total points determine your rank tier:

| Tier | Points Required | Multiplier |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| Bronze | 100 - 999 | 1.0× - 1.1× |
| Silver | 1,000 - 9,999 | 1.2× - 1.3× |
| Gold | 10,000 - 99,999 | 1.4× - 1.6× |
| Platinum | 100,000+ | 2.0× - 2.2× |

<Tip>
Higher tiers unlock multipliers that boost your future point earnings.
</Tip>

## Leaderboard

The leaderboard ranks all participants by total points:

- **Top 3** — Featured on the podium with special recognition
- **Top 100** — Visible on the main leaderboard
- **Everyone else** — Can find their rank by connecting wallet

<Card title="View the leaderboard" icon="trophy" href="https://slinq.meme/leaderboard">
  See where you rank and who's leading
</Card>

## Season Rewards

At the end of each season:

1. **Final rankings calculated** — Based on total points
2. **Rewards distributed** — On-chain distribution to top performers
3. **New season begins** — Points reset, new competition starts

<Warning>
Points reset at the end of each season. Claim your rewards before the new season begins.
</Warning>
